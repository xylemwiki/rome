Dominion Rome

Dominion Rome was a prototype expansion which would have been the 17th Dominion Expansion. This set introduced no new features, but brought back Events. The set had 4 Kingdom cards and 2 Events. The expansion icon is .


Dominion Rome was originally known as Roman Dominion and included Shillings, Instant cards, and Subdues which acted as attacking events. These functions were eventually deprecated in favor of more simple features. Instants are similar to Shadow cards, but are not a part of the deck or discard and are playable at-will. Instants were bought with Shillings.
